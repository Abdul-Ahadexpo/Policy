Mudaraba Business Policy

See Our Site

1. Business Overview:

We specialize in selling Beyblade and anime merchandise. Our mission is to provide high-quality products to anime and Beyblade enthusiasts while adhering to Islamic finance principles.

2. Investor-Mudarib Relationship:

  • Mudarib: Rasa Muntasir, Abdullah Nayem and Nazim, the entrepreneur managing the business.
  • Rab-ul-Mal (Investors): Individuals interested in investing in our business.

3. Profit Sharing:

Investor's Share: 40% of the profits. Mudarib's Share: 60% of the profits. The profit-sharing ratio is fixed and agreed upon at the start of the agreement.

4. Capital Recovery Terms:

  • One-Time Investment: Profits are shared until the agreement ends.
  • Long-Term Investment: If the investor needs their capital back, they must provide a one-month notice. During this month, no profit will be shared as the capital is being returned.

5. Loss Policy:

In compliance with Mudaraba principles: Investors bear monetary losses proportional to their investment. The Mudarib (entrepreneur) bears no financial loss but loses their time and effort.

6. Duration of Agreement:

The Mudaraba agreement lasts for a fixed term, until project completion, or until the investor opts to withdraw. Early withdrawal requires a one-month notice period, during which no profit will be shared.

7. Transparency and Reporting:

Profits and expenses are tracked and reported through our website and Google Sheets. Monthly and quarterly financial reports will be provided. Reports can also be generated upon request.

8. Payment Methods:

Profit distributions and capital returns will be paid through bKash or cash, as per the investor's preference.

9. Withdrawal or Termination Clause:

Both the investor and the Mudarib can withdraw from the agreement. If the withdrawal occurs after the funds have been utilized, the capital will be returned within one month. During the notice period, no profit will be shared.

10. Religious Compliance:

This business operates strictly in adherence to Islamic finance principles. No interest (Riba) or prohibited activities are involved. All transactions and activities are Halal and comply with Islamic guidelines.

11. Dispute Resolution:

Any disputes will be resolved amicably through mutual discussions. If required, a neutral third party with expertise in Islamic finance will mediate.
